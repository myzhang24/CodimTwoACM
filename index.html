CodimTwoACM :: CodimTwoACM

CodimTwoACM -- Codimension two ACM subvarieties of P^n

Description

Let X be a be codimension two ACM subvariety of P^n. Let R_X be the coordinate ring of X, its free resolution over the polynomial ring R is given by the Hilbert-Burch complex.

This package generates codimension two ACM subvarieties of P^n of all possible formats. By format we mean the degrees of the forms in the Hilbert-Burch matrix, which is equivalently given by the graded Betti numbers of R_X.

Since R_X has high depth, we may quotient by successive linear forms and reduce to points in P^2 without changing the Betti numbers.

Let Z be a zero dimensional scheme of degree d in P^2. The first numerical difference of the Hilbert function H(Z,n)-H(Z,n-1) is called a Castelnuovo function of degree d.

The method castelnuovo(ZZ) (missing documentation) generates all Castelnuovo functions of a given degree.

i1 : L = castelnuovo 6

o1 = {{1, 2, 2, 1}, {1, 2, 1, 1, 1}}

o1 : List

The method hilbertBurch(List) (missing documentation) generates all Hilbert-Burch degree matrices corresponding to a given Castelnuovo function.

i2 : D = hilbertBurch L#0

o2 = {| 2 |, | 1 0 |}
      | 3 |  | 2 1 |
             | 3 2 |

o2 : List
